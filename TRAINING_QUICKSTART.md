## âš™ï¸ Understanding Trading Rules

The simulator enforces **realistic trading rules** to teach you discipline:

### 1. Commission (Default: 0.1%)
**What it means**: Every trade costs money, even if you break even on price.

**Example**: 
- Buy 1 Gold @ $2,050 = $2,050 + $2.05 commission = **$2,052.05 total**
- Sell 1 Gold @ $2,050 (same price!) = $2,050 - $2.05 commission = **$2,047.95 received**
- **Net Loss: $4.10 from commissions alone!**

**Lesson**: Trade less frequently, make each trade count.

### 2. Minimum Time Gap (Default: 5 minutes)
**What it means**: You must wait 5 minutes between trades.

**Why**: Prevents emotional overtrading. Professional traders don't react to every price tick.

**If Blocked**: You'll see "â° Wait X more minutes" with explanation.

### 3. Max Position Size (Default: 50% of capital)
**What it means**: You can't put more than 50% of your initial capital into one trade.

**Example**: With $5,000 capital, you can't spend more than $2,500 on a single asset.

**Why**: Diversification and risk management. Never "all in" on one bet.

### 4. Cooldown After Loss (Default: OFF)
**What it means**: Optional rule that makes you wait after losing trades.

**Why**: Prevents "revenge trading" - trying to immediately win back losses.

**To Enable**: Go to âš™ï¸ Settings and adjust the cooldown slider.
